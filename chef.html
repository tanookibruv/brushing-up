Let's Make French Toast!

  1. butter
  2. bread
  3. eggs
  4. vanilla extract
  5. powdered sugar

Let's Put it Together

  1. 1: Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a tablespoon of vanilla extract, and stir them
  2. 2: Dip a slice of bread in the egg batter and coat both sides
  3. 3: Heat a pan to cook the bread, slap a tablespoon of butter on the pan
  4. 4: Once the butter is melted start cooking your egg coated bread
  5. 5: Cook each side until you get a golden brown
  6. 6: Once you put your toast on a plate sprinkle powdered sugar
